# Product Context - Patee Family Clothing Store

## Why This Project Exists

### Market Problem
- Vietnamese families need convenient access to coordinated family clothing
- Traditional clothing stores often lack family-focused product lines
- Online shopping for family clothing is fragmented across different categories
- Difficulty finding matching outfits for family events and occasions

### Solution Vision
Patee provides a unified platform where Vietnamese families can:
- Shop for coordinated family clothing in one place
- Find age-appropriate styles for all family members
- Access promotional deals and new arrivals easily
- Experience a modern, user-friendly shopping interface

## Target Audience

### Primary Users
1. **Vietnamese Parents (Cha/Mẹ)**
   - Age: 30-50 years
   - Shopping for themselves and children
   - Value quality, style, and family coordination
   - Comfortable with online shopping

2. **Vietnamese Grandparents (Ông/Bà)**
   - Age: 50+ years
   - Traditional preferences with modern convenience
   - Shopping for family events and daily wear
   - May need assistance with online shopping

3. **Vietnamese Children (Con Trai/Con Gái)**
   - Age: 5-18 years
   - Parents/caregivers shop on their behalf
   - Need age-appropriate, comfortable clothing

### User Personas
- **Busy Mom**: Needs quick access to family clothing, values convenience and style
- **Fashion-Conscious Dad**: Wants modern, coordinated family looks
- **Traditional Grandparent**: Prefers classic styles with easy online access
- **Young Family**: Budget-conscious, looking for deals and new arrivals

## User Experience Goals

### Shopping Experience
- **Discovery**: Easy browsing by family member categories
- **Selection**: Clear product information with color options
- **Promotion**: Attractive sales and new arrival sections
- **Convenience**: Mobile-friendly, fast-loading interface

### Emotional Connection
- **Family Unity**: Products that bring families together
- **Love Expression**: "Wear The Love" tagline emphasizes emotional connection
- **Trust**: Reliable product quality and fair pricing
- **Joy**: Pleasant shopping experience with beautiful design

## Product Categories

### Family Demographics
1. **Ông (Grandfather)**: Classic, sophisticated styles
2. **Bà (Grandmother)**: Elegant, comfortable clothing
3. **Cha (Father)**: Modern, versatile menswear
4. **Mẹ (Mother)**: Stylish, practical womenswear
5. **Con Trai (Son)**: Active, comfortable boys' clothing
6. **Con Gái (Daughter)**: Cute, fashionable girls' clothing

### Special Collections
- **Đồ Đôi (Couple Sets)**: Matching outfits for couples
- **Đồ Gia Đình (Family Sets)**: Coordinated family clothing
- **Mẹ Bé (Mother & Child)**: Matching mother-child outfits

## Business Model
- Direct-to-consumer e-commerce
- Vietnamese market focus
- Family-oriented product positioning
- Promotional pricing strategy
- Seasonal collections and sales

## Success Metrics
- User engagement with product categories
- Conversion rates on promotional sections
- Mobile vs desktop usage patterns
- Family set purchase rates
- Customer satisfaction with product coordination
